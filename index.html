Ovon Technology

Intern [Embedded Systems R&D]

Ovon Technology is an IoT-focused consulting company from Manchester. They help external clients by designing embedded systems to solve their problems but also develop a range of products as part of their own brand "Ovon Home", concentrated around intelligent heating (radiator thermostats, room thermostats, boiler controllers). During my internship, I have been working mainly with Espressif ESP32 MCU and ESP-IDF. I wrote and modified firmware in C and used Git and GitHub. I also used DMM, oscilloscope, logic analyzer and adjustable power supply on a daily basis. My tasks were both firmware and hardware related, and a number of them are listed below:
  • Developing proof-of-concepts, prototypes and demo setups (e.g. building a WiFi Hub with MQTT functionalities for multiple BLE devices; creating a simple SPI-based driver and testing the new BLDC motor driver IC, developing firmware for Infrared (IrDA) data exchange modules for the RF Shield Box).
  • Testing new firmware functionalities (e.g. ESP32-C3 Low Power Modes, ESP32 BLE Mesh capabilities).
  • Ordering parts (based on BOM), soldering (THT/SMD: soldering iron, hot air rework station, reflow oven), assembling and testing new board designs
  • Preparing boiler test setups (EBUS/OpenTherm Protocols, sensors, boiler behaviour simulation)
